MHS Global

MHS GLOBAL was created to contribute to the major challenge currently faced by companies that have packaging as part of its value chain: inovation, sustentability and cost reduction.

MHS GLOBAL offers engineering consulting services in the areas of packaging materials and machinery to meet customers’ needs in the whole packaging value chain.  

Mission

Contribute to increase the competitive advantage of companies where packaging has a strategic position in the results, acting on every link of the value chain involved.
